What is a Recurring Deposit Returns Calculator?

A recurring deposit returns calculator is an online financial tool designed to estimate your RD investment returns accurately. With our RD calculator with compound interest, you can calculate RD maturity amount instantly, helping Indian investors plan savings smarter using quarterly compounding projections.

How to Use This RD Calculator

  1. Enter your monthly RD investment to calculate RD maturity amount (₹500 to ₹10 crore)
  2. Select duration type (Years or Months)
  3. Enter your investment duration (1 to 35 years or 2 to 11 months)
  4. Input your expected annual interest rate (1% to 30%)
  5. View your estimated returns instantly just below

How Our RD Calculator with Compound Interest Works

This RD calculator applies the compound interest formula with quarterly compounding, which is widely used for most recurring deposits in India. The formula followed is: M = R × [(1 + i)^n - 1] / (1 - (1 + i)^(-1/3)), where M represents the maturity value, R is the monthly contribution, i is the quarterly interest rate (annual rate divided by 4), and n stands for the total quarters. This method ensures accurate projections of your RD maturity amount by reflecting the true impact of compounding.
