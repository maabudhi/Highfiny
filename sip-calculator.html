What is an SIP Calculator?

An SIP calculator is a free, powerful tool designed to help you estimate potential returns from your mutual fund investments. As the best SIP calculator for mutual funds, it allows you to input your monthly SIP amount, investment duration (up to 50 years), and expected rate of return to see how your wealth could grow over time. With features like the SIP calculator with annual increment and SIP returns calculator with step-up, it’s ideal for Indian investors using our monthly SIP return calculator India to plan long-term financial goals. Unlike basic tools, our SIP mutual fund growth calculator highlights potential profits, making it the perfect SIP calculator for long-term investment to achieve wealth creation with confidence.

How SIP Returns are Calculated?

This SIP Calculator uses compound interest and monthly compounding to provide reliable projections of your investment growth. The formula is: FV = P × [((1 + r)^n - 1) / r] × (1 + r), where FV represents the future value of your investment, P is the fixed monthly payment, r is the monthly rate of return (annual return divided by 12), and n is the total number of months. If your SIP contains an annual step-up or increment, the calculator accounts for the percentage increase each year before using the formula, ensuring an accurate prediction of your wealth accumulation.
